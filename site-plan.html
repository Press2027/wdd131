Hospital Management System - Website Planning Document

Site Name

Pressscare Hospital Management System

I chose this name because it represents a system that directly connects patients, doctors, and administrative staff for efficient healthcare operations. It communicates professionalism and reliability—key qualities for medical systems.

Optional domain availability: presscare.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this Hospital Management System website is to provide a centralized platform where administrators, doctors, nurses, and patients can access essential hospital information. The site will allow users to check doctor availability, appointment schedules, department details, patient services, and contact information. This system improves the efficiency of hospital operations and enhances patient experience.

Scenarios

  • “How can I book an appointment with a specific doctor?”
  • “Where can I find information about hospital departments and their services?”
  • “How do I contact the hospital for emergency or urgent care?”

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #0066CC (used for titles, navigation bar, buttons)

Secondary Color: #00A86B (used for highlights, icons, call-to-action areas)

Background Color: #F8FBFF (used for the page background to maintain a clean medical look)

Text Color: #222 (used for all body text)

Typography

  • Oswald — used for headings (H1, H2, H3) to create a bold, professional look
  • Open Sans — used for body text for easy readability and modern design
